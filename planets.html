Information on Planets

Mercury

The smallest and fastest planet in our solar system, and it's also the closest to the Sun.

Venus

The warmest planet in our solar system, and also known as Earth's sister due to their similar size and mass.

Mars

Mars, also known as the "Red Planet", has a cold desert-like world with a thin atmosphere.

Jupiter

The closest gas giant Jupiter, is the largest and most massive planet in our solar system with its signature red spot.

Saturn

The second gas giant Saturn, is mainly compose of hydrogen and helium, and it's famous for its prominent rings.

Uranus

The ice giant has a unqiue axial tilt that appear to rotate almost on its side and has rings like saturn ,but more faint in appearence.

Neptune

The farthest known planet form the sun, this ice giant is known for its deep blue coloring with unqiue features like its strong winds and the Great Dark Spot
